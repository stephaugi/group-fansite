Restaurant Recommendations

Choose Your Own Taco Adventure

Nov 11, 2025

Barrio Tacos

Various locations in Ohio, Michigan, Massachusetts, and New Hampshire
Our favorite: 15527 Madison Ave, Lakewood, OH 44107, USA

Barrio is one of those restaurants that makes dining out feel like a creative experience. Known for its build-your-own tacos, Barrio lets you customize every detail—from the shell and protein to the toppings and sauces—so no two meals are ever the same. The ingredients are always fresh, and the menu offers great variety, whether you're craving something classic like seasoned beef or adventurous like the spicy chorizo or tequila-lime shrimp. The chips and queso are also a must-have starter—crispy, warm, and perfect for sharing.

Beyond the tacos, Barrio's sides and specials round out the experience. Their rice bowls and nachos are hearty and full of flavor, and you can load them up with the same fresh toppings that make their tacos so popular. It's the kind of place that works just as well for a casual lunch as it does for a night out with friends.

Of course, no trip to Barrio feels complete without one of their signature margaritas. The strawberry is always a favorite—sweet, refreshing, and perfectly balanced—but every once in a while, the jalapeño margarita hits the spot with its unexpected kick. Together with the flavorful food and lively atmosphere, Barrio is a local favorite that never disappoints.

We particularly love these dishes and ingredients...

  • Green goddess shell - flour soft + corn hard + queso + guac
  • Cola Marinated Steak
  • Thai Chili Tofu
  • Pineapple Salsa
  • Avocado Ranch

A Deli Worth Melting For

Oct 31, 2025

Joe's Deli

19215 Hilliard Blvd, Rocky River, OH 44116

On a hot summer Saturday afternoon, my sister, her boyfriend, and I stopped by Joe's Deli in Rocky River for our first visit—and it didn't take long for us to understand why it's such a local favorite. Despite the heat, my sister's boyfriend ordered a bowl of chicken noodle soup, which at first seemed like an odd choice for the weather. But one spoonful changed our minds—the broth was rich, comforting, and full of flavor, the kind of homemade taste that makes you wish you'd ordered your own bowl.

I went with the veggie melt sandwich, which was perfectly toasted and packed with fresh, flavorful vegetables and melty cheese. My sister's cranberry chicken salad was just as impressive—light, refreshing, and full of that perfect sweet-and-savory balance. Everything tasted so fresh, and the portions were generous without being overwhelming.

What really stood out, though, was the warm, welcoming vibe of Joe's Deli. The staff was friendly, the service was quick, and even on a busy afternoon, the atmosphere felt relaxed and inviting. By the end of the meal, we were already talking about what we'd try next time. Joe's Deli has definitely earned a spot on our list of go-to places in Rocky River.

We particularly love these dishes...

  • Fresh Fruit Salad (with a Cranberry Orange muffin on the side)
  • Fried Chicken Sandwich
  • Cranberry Chicken Salad
  • Chicken Noodle Soup
  • Corned Beef Sandwich on Rye bread

From Pirouettes to Pasta: The Perfect Post-Class Retreat

Aug 7, 2025

Sofia's Kitchen + Bar

17621 Detroit Ave Lakewood Ohio 44107

Sofia's Kitchen in Lakewood has quickly become one of my favorite post-dance class spots. After ballet or tap, my friends and I love grabbing a seat at the bar—it's the perfect mix of cozy and elegant, with friendly bartenders who always have a great recommendation. The “Fire on the Mountain” cocktail is a standout: bold, spicy, and perfectly balanced, it has just the right amount of heat to make each sip exciting without overpowering the flavors. It's become a bit of a ritual for us to order one after class.

To start, the rotating cheese selection is always a treat. Each visit brings something new—creamy, tangy, and often paired with thoughtful accompaniments like honey, nuts, or seasonal fruit. It's the kind of appetizer that feels indulgent but never heavy, setting the tone for the rest of the meal.

The Campanelle pasta is another highlight—tender, curly noodles coated in a rich sauce that's clearly made with care. Whether paired with fresh vegetables or a savory protein, it's comfort food elevated to something special. Between the inventive cocktails, delicious food, and relaxed yet refined atmosphere, Sofia's Kitchen is the perfect place to unwind and savor the night.

We particularly love these dishes and drinks...

  • Fire on the Mountain
  • Pumpkin Spice Nott-A
  • Artisanal Cheese Selection
  • Campanelle Pasta
  • Sesame Noodle Bowl
