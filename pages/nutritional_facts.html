The Most Nutrient Dense Foods

Commonly hailed as superfoods or power foods, these nutrient-packed champions, with their nutrient density scores, lead the charge, both in terms of popularity and the nutrients they contain:

  • Spinach 100%
  • Mussels 91%
  • Liver 89%
  • Broccoli 88%
  • Mackerel 87%
  • Salmon 85%
  • Shrimp/prawns 83%
  • Chicken thigh 72%
  • Sirloin steak 71%
  • Whole egg 71%
  • Lupins 69%
  • Cottage cheese 66%
